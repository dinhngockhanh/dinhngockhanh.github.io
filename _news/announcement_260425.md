We participated in the event <a href="https://zuckermaninstitute.columbia.edu/saturday-science-zuckerman">Saturday Science: From A to ZI</a>, organized by BioBus.
Peter, Khanh and other scientists at IICD prepared three games to teach children in the Harlem neighborhood about how nucleotides form the DNA double helix, fragment mapping in DNA-sequencing and the Mendelian syndromes.
We will participate again at the next BioBus Saturday Science event!
